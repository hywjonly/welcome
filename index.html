Research interests

My research interests lie in discrete geometry, a little bit about computational geometry, a little bit about functional analysis (topics on convex cones or relates to polytopes) and a little bit about theoretical computer science. In discrete geometry, I mostly work on polytopes. Topics about polytopes that I have been focusing on and have made progress are: the decomposability of polytopes and f-vectors of polytopes.
