Research interests

My research interests lie in discrete geometry, a little bit about computational geometry, a little bit about functional analysis (topics on convex cones or relates to polytopes) and a little bit about theoretical computer science. In discrete geometry, I mostly work on polytopes. Topics about polytopes that I have been focusing on and have made progress are: the decomposability of polytopes and f-vectors of polytopes.

Publications

Lower bound results for conditionally decomposable polytopes (joint work with David Yost). accepted. arXiv [Description. We're interested in giving the existence of conditionally decomposable polytopes in higher dimensions, and also the minimum number of vertices of a conditionally decomposable polytope. While the latter is not achieved, we narrowed it down to [3d-4, 4d-4]. I enjoyed the discovery of this paper a lot.]

Polytopes with low excess degree (joint work with David Yost and Guillermo Pineda-Villavicencio). accepted. arXiv [Description. It is known that polytopes with excess degree in the range [1, d-3] don't exist. We push it further that polytopes with excess degree in the range [d+3, 2d-7] don't exist.]

A refined lower bound theorem for d-polytopes with at most 2d vertices (joint work with David Yost and Guillermo Pineda-Villavicencio) arXiv [Description. This paper serves as an important preliminary result for the paper "A lower bound theorem for d-polytopes with at most 3d-1 vertices" below.]

A lower bound theorem for d-polytopes with 2d+2 vertices (joint work with Guillermo Pineda-Villavicencio, Aholiab Tritama and David Yost). arXiv [Description. As the title suggests. My contribution to this paper is minor, mainly in the equality analysis on the facets.]

A lower bound theorem for d-polytopes with at most 3d-1 vertices (joint work with Guillermo Pineda-Villavicencio). arXiv [Description. The lower bound on the number of k-faces of a d-polytope with d+s (2 ≤ s ≤ d) vertices is known, this is Grünbaum's lower bound conjecture, which was proved by Xue. We give the lower bound on the number of k-faces of a d-polytope with 2d+s (1 ≤ s ≤ d-1) vertices. This paper is lengthy.]

Characterization of decomposable d-polytopes with 2d+2, 2d+3 vertices, where d > = 4, d > = 5, respectively (joint work with David Yost) in preparation, to appear on arXiv.
